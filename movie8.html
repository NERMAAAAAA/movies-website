Love Rosie

"Love Rosie" is a heartwarming romantic comedy that follows the life of Rosie Dunne and her best friend Alex Stewart, who share a deep bond throughout their childhood. However, when life and circumstances pull them in different directions, they find themselves constantly missing out on the love they could have had.

From missed opportunities to awkward situations, the film chronicles their journey over the years as they navigate love, friendship, and heartbreak. *Love Rosie* is a tale of destiny, timing, and the hope that true love will find its way, even if it takes a lifetime.
