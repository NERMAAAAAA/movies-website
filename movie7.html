Insidious

"Insidious" is a chilling supernatural horror film that follows a family as they struggle to protect their son, Dalton, who falls into a mysterious coma. Soon, they discover that Dalton's condition is linked to a terrifying paranormal force that threatens their lives.

As they delve deeper into the eerie realm known as "The Further," a dark dimension inhabited by malevolent spirits, the family must confront their worst fears to save Dalton and themselves. Filled with suspense, jump scares, and a haunting atmosphere, *Insidious* delivers a terrifying journey into the unknown.
