Call

"Call" is a chilling thriller that transcends time. The film follows two women, Seo-yeon and Young-sook, connected by a mysterious phone call despite living 20 years apart. As they form a bond, their actions lead to unexpected consequences that change their lives forever.

This gripping story explores the butterfly effect and raises questions about fate and moral choices, creating a heart-pounding experience with twists and turns. With haunting performances and intense cinematography, "Call" is a must-watch for fans of suspenseful storytelling.
